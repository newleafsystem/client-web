Iron Condor: Range-Bound Income

Profit from low volatility by selling both a call spread and put spread. Collect premium when the stock stays within a predicted range. Best for sideways markets with defined support and resistance.

Theta Decay Time works in your favor—profit as options expire worthless

High Win Rate Historically 65-75% probability of profit with proper setup
