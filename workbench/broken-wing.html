Broken Wing Butterfly: Asymmetric Edge

A modified butterfly with one wider wing — collect a credit on entry and eliminate risk entirely on the narrow side. Best for stocks with a clear gamma wall and a wider expected band where iron condors are too tight.

💰
Credit Entry Get paid to enter — net credit on most setups
🛡️
Zero Risk Side Narrow wing → no loss if price runs that way
🎯
Gamma Wall Body anchors at the strongest open-interest wall
📐
Wider Bands Works where condors don't fit — band > 10%

How it works

  • Sell two ATM/near-ATM options at the body strike
  • Buy one option at the narrow wing (close to the body)
  • Buy one option at the wide wing (further from the body)
  • Pick wider wing on the side you're least worried about
  • Profits maximised at the body strike at expiration
