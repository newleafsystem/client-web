📊 Gamma Wall Analysis
Gamma walls reveal where market makers must hedge their options exposure. These strikes act as invisible magnets — the stock tends to gravitate toward them and bounce between the put wall (floor) and call wall (ceiling). Use these levels to position your short strikes where dealers provide natural support.
