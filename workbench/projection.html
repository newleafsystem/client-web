How to explain this to investors

We are not promising every trade will win. We are designing the process so losses are survivable.

With 100–200 trades per year, the result depends less on one trade and more on the repeatability of the edge. A 60–65% win rate can be powerful only when the losses are capped, position size is controlled, and trades are diversified.

Formula Expected value = win rate × average win − loss rate × average loss
Current scenario 62% × 1.2% − 38% × 1.0% = 0.36% expected value per trade
Safe approach Small edge × many trades × capped risk = system, not gambling.
